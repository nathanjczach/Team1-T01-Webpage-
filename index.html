Our Mission

ToysForDogs is dedicated to ensuring every dog, regardless of their circumstances, has access to safe, engaging toys that promote physical health, mental stimulation, and pure canine happiness.

Founded in 2020, we believe that play is not a luxury but a fundamental right for all dogs. Through community partnerships, shelter donations, and educational programs, we work to bridge the toy gap affecting dogs in underserved communities and rescue organizations.

Our Programs

Shelter Support Initiative

Making Shelters More Playful

We partner with over 150 animal shelters nationwide to provide monthly toy deliveries. Each shelter receives a curated selection of durable, safe toys appropriate for dogs of all sizes and temperaments.

What We Provide:
  • Chew toys for anxious dogs
  • Interactive puzzle toys for mental stimulation
  • Plush comfort toys for scared newcomers
  • Tug toys for playful pups

Impact: In 2024, we delivered over 25,000 toys to shelters across the country.

Community Toy Banks

Free Toys for Families in Need

Our Community Toy Banks operate in 30 cities, providing free toys to families who may struggle to afford enrichment items for their beloved pets. No questions asked, no income verification required.

How It Works:

Families can visit any of our toy bank locations once per month and select up to three toys for their dogs. We also offer seasonal special events where families can receive holiday-themed toys and treats.

Find a Toy Bank Near You

Visit our locations page to find a Community Toy Bank in your area. New locations opening quarterly!

Play Education Workshops

Teaching the Power of Play

Our certified trainers conduct free workshops for dog owners and shelter staff, teaching the importance of play in canine development and behavior. Topics include toy safety, appropriate play styles, and using toys for training.

We have trained over 5,000 dog owners and shelter volunteers since our program launch in 2021.

Ways to Donate

Every Contribution Makes Tails Wag

Monetary Donations

Financial contributions help us purchase toys in bulk, reducing costs and maximizing impact. A donation of just $25 provides toys for five shelter dogs.

Toy Drives

Host a toy drive at your workplace, school, or community center. We provide all the materials you need including collection boxes, flyers, and guidelines for safe toy selection.

Corporate Partnerships

We work with pet product manufacturers and retailers to receive donated overstock, discontinued items, and factory seconds that are still perfectly safe and fun.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of volunteers who sort donations, staff toy banks, and help at events. Flexible schedules available.

Impact Stories

Bella's Story: From Fearful to Playful

Happy Tails Animal Shelter, Ohio

Bella arrived at Happy Tails severely neglected and afraid of humans. The shelter staff used our donated plush toys to help build her confidence. Within weeks, Bella was carrying her favorite squeaky duck everywhere.

"The toys from ToysForDogs were instrumental in Bella's rehabilitation," said Sarah, shelter manager. "She's now been adopted by a wonderful family and is thriving."

Community Impact: Riverside Toy Bank

Supporting 200 Families Monthly

Our Riverside location serves families in a community where pet stores are scarce and expensive. Maria, a single mother of three with two rescue dogs, shares her experience:

"My kids and dogs are so happy when we visit the toy bank. It's not just about the toys - it's knowing someone cares about our whole family, including our furry members."

Shelter Success: 40% Increase in Adoptions

Paws & Claws Rescue, California

After partnering with ToysForDogs, Paws & Claws saw a remarkable change. Dogs with toys in their kennel photos were adopted 40% faster than those without.

"A dog playing with a toy shows potential adopters the dog's personality," explained Director Mike Chen. "It makes them real, not just another shelter dog."
